LAVINDA EYE SPECIALIST HOSPITAL AND LASER CENTRE

OUR VISION

To offer Quality eye care services with strict adherence to best practice and drive for excellence at all times.

OUR MISSION

1. To restore good vision to the blind and visually impaired.
2. To preserve vision and help to avert blindness. These shall be achieved through:

  • Provision of qualitative eye care with modern technology.
  • Public enlightenment.
  • Collaboration with other individuals, institutions and organizations with similar goals.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Lavinda eye specialist hospital is a medical outfit, committed to offering specialized eye care services to our clients in order to promote good vision. It was established in 2007 with a drive to be the best private eye specialist service providers in Nigeria and beyond the shores of Nigeria, in terms of quality, speed and using the best and most efficient technology. Our aim is to offer quality eye care services to improve vision at an affordable cost with state-of-the-art equipment and very optimal service delivery approach.

INTRODUCTION

Lavinda has taken eye care services to a whole new level in the city of Port Harcourt, with the acquisition of the most recent eye care technology gadgets and very friendly/competent staff. The hospital has carved a niche in the area of high quality services delivery since inception. This has led to the sporadic growth in clientele, personnel and infrastructure of the hospital. The clientele of the hospital spans corporate, family and private patients. Lavinda currently services many corporate interests in Port Harcourt and outside Port Harcourt.

OUR SERVICES

As an eye care service provider with a hunch for excellence at all times, with accuracy and proper satisfaction of the client, we offer the underlisted services (this list is by no means exhaustive):

Eye Surgeries

  • Cataract extraction with intraocular lens implantation (small incision sutureless cataract surgeries, phacoemulsification cataract surgeries, YAG laser posterior capsulotomy for secondary cataracts).
  • Glaucoma surgeries (Trabeculectomy filtration surgeries, Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty, Laser Peripheral iridotomy, Laser suturelysis post trabeculectomy).
  • Pterygium excision surgeries which include conjunctival autograft.
  • Chalazion surgeries
  • Traumatic eye conditions
  • General eye surgeries

Medical Eye Condition

  • Glaucoma
  • Hypertensive eye disease
  • Diabetic eye disease
  • Hyphema
  • Maculopathies
  • Retinopathies

Optical Services

  • Refraction
  • Dispensing of glasses
  • Quality frames
  • Quality contact lenses

Specialized general eye check-up
We shall soon commence Laser photocoagulation for retinal cases.

  • Neon Diamond-YAG laser machine
  • Selective laser trabeculoplasty machine
  • Phacoemulsification machine
  • Anterior vitrectomy machine
  • Digital ophthalmic work station
  • Digital Central visual field Analyzer

  • A mini side laboratory for tests (Blood sugar level, HIV screening, Urinalysis, etc.)

  • Biometric machine

  • Phoropter
  • Drilling machine
