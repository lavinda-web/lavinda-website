Lavinda Eye Specialist Hospital and Laser Centre

Our Vision

To offer quality eye care services with strict adherence to best practices and a drive for excellence at all times.

Our Mission

1. To restore good vision to the blind and visually impaired.
2. To preserve vision and help avert blindness through:

  • Provision of qualitative eye care with modern technology.
  • Public enlightenment.
  • Collaboration with individuals, institutions, and organizations with similar goals.

Executive Summary

Lavinda Eye Specialist Hospital is committed to offering specialized eye care services to our clients, promoting good vision. Established in 2007, we strive to be the best private eye specialist in Nigeria and beyond, in terms of quality, speed, and modern technology. Our goal is to provide top-tier eye care at affordable costs, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment and an optimal service-delivery approach.

Introduction

Lavinda has elevated eye care in Port Harcourt by acquiring the latest technological tools and friendly, competent staff. Since inception, we have built a reputation for high-quality service delivery, fueling growth in our clientele, personnel, and infrastructure. Our clientele includes corporate entities, families, and private individuals. We currently serve many corporate interests both in and out of Port Harcourt.

Our Services

As an eye care provider dedicated to excellence and accuracy, we offer the following (not exhaustive) services:

Eye Surgeries

  • Cataract Extraction: Small-incision sutureless procedures, phacoemulsification, and YAG laser for secondary cataracts.
  • Glaucoma: Trabeculectomy filtration surgeries, Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty, Laser Peripheral iridotomy.
  • Pterygium Excision: Includes conjunctival autograft procedures.
  • Chalazion Surgeries, Traumatic Eye Conditions, General Eye Surgeries

Medical Eye Conditions

  • Glaucoma, Hypertensive Eye Disease, Diabetic Eye Disease, Hyphema, Maculopathies, Retinopathies

Optical Services

  • Refraction & Dispensing of Glasses
  • Quality Frames
  • Contact Lenses

We offer specialized eye check-ups and will soon commence Laser Photocoagulation for retinal cases.
