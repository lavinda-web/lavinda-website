LAVINDA EYE SPECIALIST HOSPITAL AND LASER CENTRE

OUR VISION

To offer Quality eye care services with strict adherence to best practice and drive for excellence at all times.

OUR MISSION

1. To restore good vision to the blind and visually impaired.
2. To preserve vision and help to avert blindness. These shall be achieved through:

  • Provision of qualitative eye care with modern technology.
  • Public enlightenment.
  • Collaboration with other individuals, institutions and organizations with similar goals.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Lavinda eye specialist hospital is a medical outfit, committed to offering specialized eye care services to our clients in order to promote good vision. It was established in 2007 with a drive to be the best private eye specialist service provider in Nigeria and beyond, in terms of quality, speed and using the best and most efficient technology. Our aim is to offer quality eye care services to improve vision at an affordable cost with state-of-the-art equipment and an optimal service-delivery approach.

INTRODUCTION

Lavinda has taken eye care services to a whole new level in Port Harcourt with the acquisition of the most recent eye care technology gadgets and very friendly, competent staff. The hospital has carved a niche in high-quality service delivery since its inception. This has led to sporadic growth in clientele, personnel, and infrastructure. The clientele spans corporate, family, and private patients, with Lavinda currently servicing many corporate interests both in and outside Port Harcourt.

OUR SERVICES

As an eye care provider dedicated to excellence and accuracy, we offer the following (this list is by no means exhaustive):

Eye Surgeries

  • Cataract extraction with intraocular lens implantation (small incision sutureless cataract surgeries, phacoemulsification cataract surgeries, YAG laser posterior capsulotomy for secondary cataracts).
  • Glaucoma surgeries (Trabeculectomy filtration surgeries, Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty, Laser Peripheral iridotomy, Laser suturelysis post trabeculectomy).
  • Pterygium excision surgeries which include conjunctival autograft.
  • Chalazion surgeries
  • Traumatic eye conditions
  • General eye surgeries

Medical Eye Condition

  • Glaucoma
  • Hypertensive eye disease
  • Diabetic eye disease
  • Hyphema
  • Maculopathies
  • Retinopathies

Optical Services

  • Refraction & Dispensing of Glasses
  • Quality Frames
  • Quality Contact Lenses

Specialized general eye check-up
We shall soon commence Laser photocoagulation for retinal cases.

EQUIPMENT

The hospital is well-equipped with modern ophthalmologic equipment for both diagnosis and treatment of eye conditions. A list of our ophthalmologic equipment:

  • Neon Diamond-YAG laser machine
  • Selective laser trabeculoplasty machine
  • Phacoemulsification machine
  • Anterior vitrectomy machine
  • Digital ophthalmic work station
  • Digital Central Visual Field Analyzer
  • An Automated air puff tonometer for checking eye pressure
  • A modern slit lamp Biomicroscope
  • An indirect Ophthalmoscope for broad fundal eye examination
  • Operation microscope for carrying out eye surgeries
  • A mini in-house laboratory for tests (Blood sugar level, HIV screening, Urinalysis, etc.)
  • Automated refractometer
  • A and B ocular ultrasound scan machine
  • Biometric machine, Keratometer, Phoropter
  • Drilling machine
  • Frame warmer machine
  • Grooving machine
  • Lens polishing machine
  • Edging machine
