Our Mission in Eye Care

Lavinda Eye Specialist Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional ophthalmic care, leveraging cutting-edge technology and patient-focused treatment. Located in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, we specialize in a wide range of services—from cataract and laser surgeries to comprehensive eye exams and low-vision support.

We offer an array of specialized services to help you see the world more clearly. From advanced surgical interventions to preventive care and patient education, we focus on personalized treatments designed to preserve and enhance vision.
